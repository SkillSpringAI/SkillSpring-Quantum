# Data Policy

## Scope
This policy governs the handling of raw exported conversation data.

## Rules
1. Raw exports are source material, not ready-made datasets.
2. Raw files should be parsed without semantic invention.
3. Full transcripts may be preserved in markdown export where useful.
4. Segmented snippets should be preferred for topic organization and dataset use.
5. Raw exported content should remain local-first unless intentionally moved elsewhere by the operator.

## Restrictions
- No automatic cloud transmission
- No hidden external processing
- No silent content rewriting beyond explicit formatting, redaction, or segmentation rules
